Welcome to the Viddat Tutorial


Step 1: Create an AWS Account

To get started, you need an AWS Account in order to generate the audio for your videos!

IMPORTANT: Our service is completely FREE but if your are not on the FREE TIER of AWS you may incurr AWS FEES

Please look at the Free Tier limits of AWS SPECIFICALLY Polly (neural speech) and Cloud Data Transfer

As of 7/17/2024 the FREE Tier consists of 1 Million characters per month of Neural TTS and 100 Gigabytes Data Transfer per month.


Step 2: Create an IAM User for AWS Keys

To start creating on VidDat, you must have valid AWS configurations for the AWS Polly Service (Text To Speech) and the S3 Service (Cloud Storage)

Follow these steps to enable these settings and to retreive your AWS Keys!


Create AWS User

Click on the drop down menu on the top right and then press on "Security credentials".

Next click on "Users" on the menu on the left.

Press "Create user", choose a name (Can be anything).

Choose "Attach policies directly", search and click for "AmazonPollyFullAccess" and "AmazonS3FullAccess".

Selecting these policies are important for security purposes as this means the keys will ONLY have access to the Text To Speech Service and Online Cloud Storage Service

Heres what the review and create page should look like, press "Create user".

Now that we have our AWS User created, lets get our Access and Secret Keys!

Choose your user

Press "Create access key"

Choose "Third-party service", confirm, and create.

Copy your keys to the profile settings page!

Step 3: Save your keys on the settings page!

Now that you have your AWS Keys copy and paste them into their respective boxes.

Choose a profile picture and profile username. These represent the image and name of the title card shown below.

Remember to press save!

You are now all set to start creating!
